                                                                                                                                            `# 🟢 WaBiri User Guide: Getting Started

Welcome to **WaBiri**! This guide will help you manage your contacts and send bulk WhatsApp messages efficiently. Whether you are a real estate agent or a small business owner, this platform is built to make your communication seamless.

---

## 1. Connecting Your WhatsApp
Before you can send any messages, you need to connect your WhatsApp account.
1.  Go to the **Instances** page in the sidebar.
2.  Click **"Create Instance"** and give it a name (e.g., "Main-Business-Phone").
3.  A **QR Code** will appear on the screen.
4.  Open WhatsApp on your phone, go to **Linked Devices**, and scan the QR code just like you do for WhatsApp Web.
5.  Once it says **"Connected"**, you are ready to go!

---

## 2. Managing Your Contacts (CRM)
WaBiri is a powerful **Contact Management System (CMS)**. It helps you keep track of thousands of leads easily.

### Importing from Excel/CSV
If you have a list of contacts in Excel:
1.  Save your file as a **CSV (Comma Separated Values)**.
2.  Go to the **Contacts** page.
3.  Click **"Import CSV"** and select your file.
4.  WaBiri will automatically read columns like *Name, Phone, Email,* and *Interest*. 
5.  **Note:** If a contact already exists, WaBiri will simply update their information instead of creating a duplicate.

### Organizing with Tags
Tags are labels (like "Hot Lead", "Buyer", "Follow-up") that help you group contacts.
*   **Add Tags:** When adding a new contact, type a tag and press Enter.
*   **Bulk Tagging:** Select multiple contacts in your list and click **"Bulk Tag"** to label them all at once.
*   **Filtering:** Use the search bar or the dropdown at the top to quickly find everyone interested in a specific property (e.g., "Filter by Source: Facebook").

---

## 3. Sending Bulk Messages (Campaigns)
This is where you send personalized messages to your list.

### Step 1: Select Recipients
Go to the **Bulk Send** page. You can choose recipients in two ways:
*   **By Tags:** Select a tag (e.g., "July Promo") to automatically message everyone in that group.
*   **Manual Selection:** Scroll through your list and check the boxes next to the names you want to include.

### Step 2: Write Your Message
Write your message in the box. 
*   **Personalization:** Use the trick `{{name}}` in your message. For example: 
    > *"Hi {{name}}, I have an update on the plots in Ruiru."*
*   WaBiri will automatically replace `{{name}}` with the actual name of each person (e.g., *"Hi John..."*, *"Hi Sarah..."*).

### Step 3: Review & Send
1.  Choose which **WhatsApp Account** you want to send from.
2.  Click **Next** to see a preview.
3.  **Safety First:** WaBiri adds a random **3 to 6-second delay** between every message. This prevents your WhatsApp account from being flagged as a bot.

---

## 4. Responding to Messages (Inbox)
When people reply to your campaign, you can talk to them directly inside WaBiri.
1.  Go to the **Inbox** page.
2.  You will see your recent chats on the left.
3.  Click a chat to read the history and reply instantly without opening your phone.

---

## 💡 Quick Tips for Success
*   **Clean your list:** Ensure phone numbers are in the international format (e.g., `2547...`) for the best delivery.
*   **Stay Personal:** People reply much more often when you use their name in the message.
*   **Monitor Stats:** After sending a campaign, check the **Campaigns** page to see how many messages were successfully delivered.
